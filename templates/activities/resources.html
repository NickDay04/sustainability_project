Cars, Vans, and Motorbikes

We get our data for your car's carbon emissions from the widely used and reliable source Carbon Footprint.

This resource is references multiple large scale companies that use their services.

Flights

We allow you to select from a list of the top 1000 airports in the world ranked by annual traffic.

We calculate the distance between the two chosen airports by using the Haversine Formula using the logitude and latitude coordinates of the chosen airports.

Finally, we calculate the carbon emissions by multiplying by the average carbon emissions of short haul vs long haul flights.

Food

We used a list of foods that have a large impact on carbon emissions. We discarded low emission foods from the dataset.

It is important to note that our list of food is non-exhaustive list of high-emission foods.
